About BIR 2316

The BIR Form 2316 is a certificate of compensation payment/tax withheld that employers provide to their employees.

This form contains:

  • Total compensation paid
  • Total tax withheld
  • Employee's tax information
  • Employer's tax information

Use this form for:

  • Annual tax filing
  • Tax refund applications
  • Loan applications
  • Government transactions
